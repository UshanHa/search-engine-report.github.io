Project Plan

Tittle

Watch Collection As A Hobby

Goal

To educate people about the quality and the procedure that goes behing on maing a perfect timepiece and to inform people about the hobby and passion that collectors have

Target Audience

People who are into history or into collecting rare pieces. Students and workers who are interested in timpieces. People who are interested in knowing about the mechanics and engineering of the movement inside the watch and the Age 10-60

Main Features

This site will explain about watch collectors passion and will showcase a famous watch which is admired by watch collectors. It will frequently asked questions and contact form

Personality

Friendly. Elegant. Designed to provide the audience an idea about the concept of wristwatches. Passionate. Enthuastic. Willing to share knowledge or inspire.

The heading color gives a warm feeling to the topic. The background color is aqua which provides relaxing and trust emotions. Orange is combination of red and yellow, a warm vibrant color with connotations of energy and flamboyance. It's less intense or aggressive than red. It gives the site a fun and bright appearance. Green highlights the picture and gives the background a soothing and relaxing feeling it also represents the dial of the watch which has a green dial watch and will atrract more people as the color on the dial has a huge attraction or fanbase and is known as "Hulk" which resembles the color green. The green color provides a clear background color for the conclusion's image and provides a clean and elegant look. The purple color ar the footer brings childish and fun concept to the site. The watch making picture at the begining will give a sense about the website and will attract more audience. The darker color around the contact forum keeps it seperated and creates a dark or end feeling.

Limiting Factors

My Target audience are watch enthuasists or people who are interested or wants yo be educated in knowing about the history of watch movements and the procedures that take place with them, my limitations would be to educate them and not to use the website for selling or buying purposes and to provide them with enough knowledge so that they can have a good idea about buying their first watch or looking for something that would be worth their price.
